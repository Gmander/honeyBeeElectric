About Honey Bee Electric LLC!

A little bit the owner


Hi my name is Christopher Daggett, founder of Honey Bee Electric. In 2009 I went to a four year vocational school. Assabett Valley Regional Vocational Technical High School. Since day one of electrical class I fell in love with the trade. I was so fascinated how electricity worked and how its applied to everyday life. I went on my own in 2017 to pursue my dream of owning an electrical company. Since YEAR FOUNDED, I’ve proudly served residential and commercial customers in New Hampshire & Massachusetts. My goal is to bring excellence in all aspects of Electrical wiring services from small renovations to large scale work, by providing prompt, high quality workmanship - at competitive rates. My approach is simple: I take the time to understand what my customers’ needs are and develop the right plan, at the right price. I pride myself on my professionalism - friendly, skilled and insured for your protection. I stand behind everything I do with my satisfaction guaranteed policy. Visit my services pages to see what I can do for you today.
