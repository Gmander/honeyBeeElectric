Tampa Bay Testing


Tampa Bay Testing will work with you to identify your strengths and problem solve ways to become the best you. Michele Durrance-Miller, PsyD, a licensed clinical psychologist in Florida, (PY6956), and her team provide their clients with a comprehensive array of diagnostic and evaluation services with a personalized approach. Dr. Durrance-Miller specializes in psychological assessments for those who struggle with ADHD, learning challenges and emotional/behavioral sturggles. Evaluations are provided for all age levels including children and adults.
